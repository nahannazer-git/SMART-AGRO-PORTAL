# User Manual - Smart Agriculture Support System

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal
1.  Navigate to [https://farmers-portal.onrender.com](https://farmers-portal.onrender.com) (or your local URL).
2.  The **Landing Page** provides an overview of the system services.
3.  Click **"Login"** or **"Register"** to proceed.

### Registration
*   Select your role: **Farmer** or **Expert**.
*   Fill in the required details (Name, Phone, Location/Qualification).
*   Create a secure password.
*   *Note: Krishi Bhavan Officers and Admins are pre-registered by the system administrator.*

---

## 2. For Farmers

### How to Check Crop Health
1.  Log in to your dashboard.
2.  Navigate to **"Crop Health"** or **"New Issue"**.
3.  Upload a clear photo of the affected leaf/plant.
4.  Select the **Crop Type** and check any visible **Symptoms** from the list.
5.  Submit the form.
6.  The AI will provide an immediate *preliminary* prediction.
7.  The issue is sent to an Expert for a confirmed diagnosis. Check **"My Reports"** later for the doctor's reply.

### How to Sell Products
1.  Go to the **"Marketplace"** tab.
2.  Click **"Add Product"**.
3.  Enter the product name (e.g., "Organic Tomatoes"), quantity, and price.
4.  Upload a photo of your produce.
5.  Click **"List Item"**. Your product is now visible to the public!

### How to Request Subsidies
1.  Go to **"Krishi Bhavan"**.
2.  Browse available seeds, fertilizers, and tools.
3.  Click **"Request"** on the item you need.
4.  Track the status of your request in the **"My Requests"** tab.

---

## 3. For Experts

### Processing Cases
1.  Log in to view your **Dashboard**.
2.  The **"Pending Issues"** section lists new cases assigned to you.
3.  Click **"View Case"** to see the farmer's upload and symptom list.
4.  Use the **"Chat"** feature to ask the farmer for more details if needed.
5.  Click **"Diagnose"** to fill out your report:
    *   Identify the disease.
    *   Prescribe treatment.
    *   Recommend products.
6.  Submit the report to close the case.

---

## 4. For Krishi Bhavan Officers

### Managing Inventory
1.  Log in to the Officer Dashboard.
2.  Go to **"Inventory"**.
3.  Click **"Add New Item"** to list subsidized resources (Seeds, Fertilizers).
4.  Update stock levels as items are distributed.

### Approving Requests
1.  Go to **"Farmer Requests"**.
2.  Review pending requests for subsidies.
3.  Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"** based on eligibility and stock availability.

### Posting Notices
1.  Go to **"Notices"**.
2.  Create a new notice for schemes, weather alerts, or meetings.
3.  This notice will appear on all Farmer dashboards in your region.
